Vocations & Formation

St. Peter Claver Community House, Obosima, Ohaji-Egbema LGA, Imo State, Nigeria

Formation Stages

  • Postulancy: Introductory period of discernment, prayer, and introduction to religious life.
  • Novitiate: Intensive year of spiritual formation, study of the vows, and deepening of the Claverian charism.
  • Philosophy & Theology Studies: Academic formation in preparation for priesthood or brotherhood.
  • Pastoral Year / Regency: Hands-on mission experience in parishes, schools, or missions.
  • Perpetual Profession & Ordination: Final commitment to the congregation and ordination to the priesthood for those called.

Admission Requirements

  • Confirmed, practicing Catholic
  • O'Level (WAEC/NECO) holders: 17–30 years
  • Degree holders: 28–39 years
  • Brotherhood candidates: up to 45 years
  • Five O'Level credits including English and Mathematics
  • Medical fitness

Our Formation Houses

Main Base: St. Peter Claver International Missionary Academy (Claverate Missionary Academy), Aguneze, Ahiara, Ahiazu Mbaise LGA, Imo State, Nigeria.

Postulancy House: St. Peter Claver Community House, Obosima, Ohaji-Egbema LGA, Imo State, Nigeria.

Application Process

Send your application to our Vocation Director via email. Include your academic qualifications, a brief testimony of your faith journey, and your contact information. Entrance exams and interviews are held periodically. Contact us for current dates.
