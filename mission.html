Our Mission, Vision & Charism

Mission Statement

To propagate and spread the Good News of Christ while living the life of St. Peter Claver. We focus on serving through community life, hospitality, education (especially for children of the poor), care for the poor, homeless, sick, and aged, retreats and seminars, and pastoral ministry in needy mission areas.

Vision Statement

To be a missionary congregation known for our love, our humility, and our radical service to the most marginalized, following the example of our patron, St. Peter Claver. We envision a community of priests and brothers who are holy, joyful, and fearless in their witness to the Gospel.

Charism & Spirituality

Our spirituality is rooted in the Claverian charism: to see Christ in the poorest, the sickest, the most forgotten. We emphasize hospitality, community life, pure Christianity, and missionary outreach. We believe that every act of mercy is an act of worship, and that the Eucharist sends us forth to serve.
