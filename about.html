Our Congregation

The Congregation of Sons of St. Peter Claver (SSPC) is a young indigenous Nigerian religious congregation of priests and brothers. We are committed to missionary service, formation of holy religious, and care for the poor, sick, and marginalized.

Our Formation Team

Brother Kelvin Umoh serves as the Secretary General and Postulancy Master. He is responsible for the initial formation of candidates, guiding them through the early stages of discernment and religious life. Under his leadership, the postulancy program focuses on spiritual growth, community living, and preparation for the novitiate.
