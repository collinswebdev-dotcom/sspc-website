⚓ Servants of the Slaves

We are an indigenous Nigerian religious congregation of priests and brothers, founded to continue the mission of St. Peter Claver — the "Slave of the Slaves." Our life is rooted in love, service, and the proclamation of the Gospel to the most marginalized.

Official Motto: "To love is to Serve"

Official Abbreviation: SSPC

Our Call

We are a young, vibrant congregation actively seeking men who are willing to serve Christ in the poor, the sick, the aged, and the forgotten. We invite you to learn more about our life, our mission, and how you might join us in building the Kingdom of God through humble service.

⚓ "Slave of the Slaves Forever" — St. Peter Claver ⚓
